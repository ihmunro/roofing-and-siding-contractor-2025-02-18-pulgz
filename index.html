Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity. Larger homes or those requiring structural repairs may take 4-5 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What types of warranties do you offer?

We provide manufacturer warranties on all materials (20-50 years depending on product) plus our own 10-year workmanship guarantee. This covers both labor and materials for complete peace of mind.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible payment plans with competitive rates. Options include 0% interest for 12 months and long-term financing up to 84 months.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We maintain full licensing and carry comprehensive insurance including liability and workers' compensation. We're happy to provide documentation upon request.

What brands and materials do you use?

We work with industry-leading manufacturers like GAF, CertainTeed, and James Hardie. All materials meet or exceed industry standards and come with extensive warranties.
